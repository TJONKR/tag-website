# Brand Decision: TAG

**Decision date:** March 2026
**Status:** Final

## The Name

**TAG** — To Achieve Greatness

Three letters. One word everyone already knows. Layers underneath for those who dig deeper.

## Why TAG

Scored 55/60 across 12 evaluation criteria — the clear winner over 8 other candidates (Vibe Club, Ground Zero, Basecamp, Scratch, Co-lab, Builders Collective, AIGEN, The New Foundation).

### Layers of meaning
- **Graffiti tag** — your mark on the world, street culture, very Amsterdam
- **"Tag, you're it"** — invitation, challenge, playful competition
- **Tag someone in** — collaboration, bringing people into the project
- **"Tag us"** — native language of building in public, community growth through sharing
- **Dog tag** — identity, belonging
- **HTML/Git tags** — code culture, builder DNA
- **Sounds like "tech"** — subtle wink that lands without explanation
- **To Achieve Greatness** — hidden acronym, ambition + action, discovered over time

### What it scored highest on
- Clever: 5/5
- Playful: 5/5
- Bar test: 5/5 ("I'm at TAG" — sounds like a place that matters)
- Amsterdam-native: 5/5 (graffiti city)
- Scales: 5/5 (room → community → movement → Europe)
- Brand system: 5/5 (generates endless language)
- Hoodie test: 5/5
- Story depth: 5/5

## The Positioning

"We're the cultural layer underneath the startup ecosystem. We attract the obsessed, creative, talented people before they're ready for their next step. We're where the energy is."

### What we are
- The upstream of the startup ecosystem
- A permanent hackathon — the energy made daily
- A talent pipeline institutions can't build themselves

### What we are not
- Not an accelerator (no cohorts, no equity)
- Not an incubator (no stages, no milestones)
- Not a coworking space (culture, not desks)
- Not a networking event (no name tags, no pitches)

### Who it's for
Builders who can't stop building. Side projects > day jobs. Craft > networking. Ship > talk. Curious, open, slightly obsessed. Fun to be around.

## The Brand System

### Events & Programs
- **TAG Night** — weekly demos and drinks
- **TAG Team** — collaborative build sessions
- **TAG Drop** — launches, when someone ships
- **TAG Line** — the newsletter

### Identity
- **Tagged** — alumni, people who've been through it
- **Tag In** — the invitation, the onboarding
- **"What's your tag?"** — what are you building?

### Verbal Hooks
- "Tag, you're it." — the challenge
- "Leave your tag." — ship something, make your mark
- "Tag us." — share what you built, we amplify
- "Tag in." — join, collaborate, step up

## The Culture

- Ship fast, learn faster
- Show your work (demos are the currency)
- Help each other
- Joy is the point (ambition and joy aren't opposites)
- Build in public

## The European Thesis

Europe has the talent and technical excellence. What we're missing is the irrational belief that we deserve to win. Celebration creates momentum. Momentum creates belief. Belief attracts capital and talent. We're the hype man Europe is missing.

## The Pitch to Partners

"You fund companies. We cultivate the people who start them. We're the talent pipeline you can't build yourself."

## The Proof

- ~60 builders at Amsterdam HQ
- 2,000+ in the broader network
- 51+ projects delivered
- 60 events per year, 6+ hackathons per year
- Partners: Techleap, Norsken, AI AM

## Domain

**tag.amsterdam** — the .amsterdam TLD exists, low adoption. The URL itself is three words of positioning.
