Spray marks as accents

SVG splatter shapes made from clusters of circles and ellipses at varying sizes (2-15px radius). Multiple opacities create depth. Overspray dots around the edges sell the spray-paint feel. Use at 10-20% opacity behind content, or stronger for standalone marks.
